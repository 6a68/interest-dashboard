Mozilla Personalization Study

Firefox is conducting a study of personalized content on the Web, and as a Test Pilot user, we’d really appreciate your help in completing this quick study.

Contents of the study

Mozilla is researching the potential for Firefox to help users experience a more personalized Web.

The study consists of two components:

  1. Topic Matching (automatically completed in background)
  2. User Survey (14 questions, 3 minutes to complete)

How it works

Firefox will choose a set of interest topics that best match the websites in your browsing history. You will then be asked to answer some questions about those topics and your browsing preferences.

After completion or 2 months (whichever comes first), the study add-on will remove itself.

Data collection

Our study is both qualitative as well as quantitive. As such, we will ask you a few questions in a survey, but will also collect some data from your browser for later studies.

What will be transmitted are:

  • Your survey responses
  • The interest topics and match strengths

No specific websites or browsing history will be sent, and no personally identifying information is collected or sent to Mozilla.
