Dashboard

The Dashboard personalizes your web experience. You can control how much of your Demographics & Interests you reveal online. If you reveal your information, the more personalized the website content will be. Firefox estimates this information based on your browsing behavior. You are always in control of this information you reveal, and you can change your personalization preferences at any time because Mozilla requires websites not to track this information with you.
